We work together

Budapest Bike Maffia

Part of our leftover food is collected by Budapest Bike Maffia, a non-profit organization of volunteer bikers, who deliver food to homeless people and families in need.

Oltalom Charity Society

We ourselves deliver the other part of our leftover food to Oltalom Charity Society who serve the needs of disadvantaged, socially abandoned, vulnerable and endangered persons.

Hungarian Food Bank Association

We deliver our used kitchen tools (porcelains, utensils, small kitchen equipment) that still working, but we no longer need them to The Hungarian Food Bank Association. The organization helps to deliver our tools to the needy.

We’re Open

We took part in We’re open’s Budapest Pride, where we presented our values. We are an open company that only judges its employees by their deeds, performances and attitude.

Hero of the city program

We were among the first ones to join the Hero of the city project. In the frames of the programme we offer discount for those who regularly donate to the Child Nutrition Foundation. With this mission we wish to cease the paradox, that Budapest is full of great and busy restaurants, meanwhile there is a lot of starving children in the country.

Responsible Gastrohero

Together with Responsible Gastrohero we have launched the Small plate program, in which we organize interactive workshops for preliminary school children about healthy diet and conscious consumption.

Next Wave Europe

We are planning and realizing our offline and online communication together with the Next Wave Europe team. We choose only quickly degradable eco-friendly material for our printed marketing materials.

Fiók Lányok

Our seasonal decoration is praising the fantasy of the Fiók Lányok and is made from degradable materials. In addition, thanks to their creativity, many used utensils is recycled into cool design objects.

Egyemmeg!

We are enthusiastic participators of the Egyemmeg! Initiative for years. In the course of this family day, Fruccola is among those who welcome the little ones with healthy dishes and free educative programs. While the whole family is having fun, no questions are left unanswered about healthy diet and environmental protection.

YouTyúk/ YouHen

Our eggs are happy eggs, as Fruccola has adopted 300 hens! By participating the program of YouTyúk, we only serve you with fresh eggs that comes from Hungarian families, hen keeper primary producers.

Design Week

In the frames of Design Week, we set our creativity free and serve our Guests with special Design Menu.

Budapest100

We are regular supporter of the Budapest100 program series. We are responsible for the good tasty bites on the opening ceremony of this outstanding cultural event, this way contributing to the respect of Budapest hundred years old buildings.

Food Revolution Day

The movement of Food Revolution Day was founded by the Jamie Oliver Foundation in order to inspire and teach people about the life quality improving benefits of healthy and fresh.
